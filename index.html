    What is it?


    UX stands for User Experience

    UI stands for User Interface



    UX focuses on ease of usability and the overall user experience. We strategize on wireframing and prototyping to ensure users can easily find what you need them to know and how your firm operates on receiving/informing clients. We find it critical to ensure user satisfaction meets expectations and excels by providing an enjoyable experience. Upon execution, we can also continue to work with you by adopting methods of collecting customer feedback to continue to ensure ease of usability.


    UI works in hand with UX design by establishing a pleasant look and feel to your product. We can help with color schemes and typography to fit with your firms branding. Haven't developed a brand? No worries, we can also work with you to provide graphic and visual design along with branding skills to help you create a look that'll help you stand out.


    UX and UI work together closely to ensure users are able to have an enjoyable experience when viewing your services and how they can receive help.



    How do we help?


    Each case will vary on needs, fields of focus, and issues you'd like to address. Below, we'll show just some of the ways in which we can help provide your clients a pleasant experience.


    1. Understanding your goals and product requirements

    We start off by getting to know your firm, lawyers, and the fields you cater to. We'd like to meet you with you to discuss your goals and what your needs are. Looking for more user engagement? More clients? Increased awareness of your product and services? We’ll also go through your product to see how it currently operates. We can also help determine your target market and competitors.


    2. Site Maps

    Next we'll work on a sitemap to layout all the components and label accordingly for ease of usability. Site maps are incredibly important and constantly evolve based on users feedback and future updates. It's one of the most important steps in the UX process. We can help new clients find you easily and be able to see and understand the services you offer.


    Example of A Site Map


    3. Journey/User Flow and Profiles

    With help from the site map, we’ll also create a user map to see the natural flow of how users go through your product and what information they will encounter. We can also create user profiles; a general profile of your target market and users (example: first time client vs. returning client). This helps us to determine information architecture (determining where to place information on your product) to help new and returning clients find the information they need easily.


    Example of a User Flow Chart

    4. Visual Design

    Of course, your product needs to stand out visually and support your branding. We can help on the UI aspect with graphic design, logo development, color schemes, typography, etc. We'll help you give the look and feel you're going for to attract your users.


    5. Testing/Customer Feedback and Reports

    We will continue to work with you by adopting and gathering methods of customer feedback and continued testing to ensure you're product is consistently providing ease of use and stays on top of visual modern trends.
